About CAREFUEL

Hi. My name is Finn, and I made CAREFUEL for a school project while learning web development. It's an interactive tool for people trying to get their learnerâ€™s license. The idea came from seeing how hard it can be to remember road rules and practicing driving concepts just from a book. I wanted to make something more fun and hands-on.

CAREFUEL uses simple animations and quizzes to show how cars move around roundabouts, intersections, and other tricky spots. You can click on the animations, try to pick the right exit, and see how well you understand the rules in a safe environment.

I built this project to practice coding, designing pages, and making a small educational tool at the same time. It's still a work in progress, but I hope it helps learners feel more confident behind the wheel.

Thanks for checking out CAREFUEL.
